What Media Traffics does

Media Traffics builds paid acquisition systems for small and mid-market businesses. Most engagements combine paid ads on Meta, Google, and YouTube with AI voice agents that qualify and book inbound leads, plus the firm's KenjiAI automation platform for CRM and follow-up. The model is performance-aligned: a base monthly fee plus a percentage of new revenue generated by the campaigns we run.

Industries served most: home services (HVAC, roofing, solar, contractors), healthcare and dental, financial services, insurance, mortgage, real estate, and direct-to-consumer e-commerce.

How we handle negative reviews

Like every agency that has served 800+ businesses over 13 years, Media Traffics has received a small number of negative reviews. Our standard practice is to respond to every one publicly and offer to resolve it directly. If you are a current or former client and have an issue you want addressed, email support@mediatraffics.com with your account name and we will respond within one business day.

We do not gate reviews and we do not pay for reviews. Every review on Trustpilot, Google, and Yelp is from a real client account. Some campaigns do not perform to client expectations. When that happens, our refund and dispute process is published at our refund policy page.
